Souhardya Das

I'm Souhardya Das, a physics and math student. Academics have taught me how to think carefully, solve problems step by step, and really care about getting things right—which is how I approach my creative projects too. Besides academics, I've started writing fiction on Wattpad. I like to focus on character-driven horror with a strong atmosphere. To me, science and storytelling are both just ways to explore complicated stuff. One uses logic, and the other uses imagination and real-life experiences.
